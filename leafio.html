Leafio

Leafio, an Android app thoughtfully tailored for farmers to ease their workload. With Leafio, farmers can simply snap photos of their plant's leaves, and the app provides instant feedback on the plant's health, identifying potential diseases and issues.

In the Main Activity, users have the flexibility to either select an image from their gallery or capture a new one using their camera. Upon choosing their preferred image, clicking the "Predict" button initiates an API call to our Flask App, hosted on Heroku. This Flask App employs a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) Model to process the uploaded image. Once the Model completes its analysis and identifies the leaf disease, the Android app receives the results in JSON format.

We utilized Retrofit to establish a server call and retrieve a response through an API (Application Programming Interface) request. Subsequently, GSON was employed to efficiently parse this response.

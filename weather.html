Weather App

Weather app uses your location to provide information about the overall weather,
humidity, wind speed, and Sunrise and Sunset times at your location.

The app uses your location to provide information such as overall weather, humidity, wind speed, and sunrise and sunset times.

Working on this project taught me how to read and use JSON objects, how to send requests, how to use GSON for parsing JSON responses, how to work with a location provider, and how to use Retrofit to make API calls.

Notes App

The app allows you to captue and prioritise ideas & projects;
making notes and never forgetting them.

The app's main features are: creating a note, editing a note, displaying all saved notes, searching specific notes with substrings, and deleting them.

Working on this project taught me a lot: Timers, Recycler View and Adapters, Room Database, Data Access Objects (DAO), LiveData, ViewModels, Storing and Accessing Data in Room Database, Search View Implementation, and so on.

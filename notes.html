Notes App

The app allows you to captue and prioritise ideas & projects;
making notes and never forgetting them.

The app offers a range of core features, including the ability to create and edit notes, view your entire collection of saved notes, perform targeted searches for specific notes using substrings, and effortlessly delete them from the list.

Working on this project expanded my knowledge in various areas, such as timers, RecyclerViews and adapters, Room Database, Data Access Objects (DAO), LiveData, ViewModels, managing data storage and access in Room Database, implementing Search View, and more.

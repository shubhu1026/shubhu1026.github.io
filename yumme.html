YumMe Recipe App

An app that allows you to discover new recipes and save them to your favourites.
Try out various recipes from the internet.

The app's main features are: adding new recipes and viewing recipe details, adding recipes to favourites, getting random recipes from the internet, and receiving notifications.

YumMe was built using the MVVM architecture. I worked with permissions and made use of third-party libraries such as Glide to load images quickly, as well as Retrofit and RxJava for API calls. I worked with the Room database to save recipe data on the device;  used Livedata and ViewModels to detect changes in the recipe data and take appropriate action. Working with Navigation Components, I was able to use multiple fragments and navigate between them seamlessly. I also learned about WorkManager and Notifications (Different types and their uses).

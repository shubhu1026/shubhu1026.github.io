About Me

Hi there — thanks for stopping by! I’m Shubham Patel, a Toronto‑based Machine Learning Engineer & Full‑Stack Developer who loves turning ideas into production‑ready software.

I grew up in India and moved to Canada to deepen my technical expertise. Since then I’ve completed two post‑graduate certificates:

  • Artificial Intelligence with Machine Learning — Humber College (2025)
  • Mobile Application Development — Centennial College (2024)

Day‑to‑day, I work with Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, and modern MLOps tooling to build and deploy models that solve real business problems—from city‑scale traffic prediction to LLM‑powered question answering. I also enjoy game development (Unity/C++) and full‑stack web & mobile apps.

When I’m not coding you’ll find me playing the keyboard, sampling Toronto’s food scene, or heading out for the occasional night of clubbing. I believe great ideas come from diverse experiences and meaningful conversations—so don’t hesitate to reach out!

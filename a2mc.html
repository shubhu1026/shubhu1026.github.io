Age to Minutes Calculator

An Android app that can calculate the number of minutes you've spent in
this world based on your birth date.

The Age to Minutes Calculator app converts our age to the number of minutes we have lived. The app makes use of the Calendar Class from the java.util package to allow the user to enter their date of birth, which is then parsed into the required format. Later, the difference in milliseconds between the current time and their birth date is calculated, converted to minutes, and displayed to the user.

Working on this app taught me the fundamentals of Android development, such as working with layouts, Toasts, onClickListeners, and so on, as well as how to use the Calendar Instance and work with dates.

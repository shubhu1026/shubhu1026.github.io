Age to Minutes Calculator

An Android app that can calculate the number of minutes you've spent in
this world based on your birth date.

The Age to Minutes Calculator app seamlessly calculates the total minutes of our lifetime based on our age. Utilizing the Calendar Class from the java.util package, users can conveniently input their date of birth, ensuring it's correctly formatted. After this step, the app expertly calculates the time difference in milliseconds between the current moment and the user's birth date, converts it into minutes, and displays the result for the user's convenience.

Developing this app provided me with a solid foundation in Android development. It encompassed essential skills like handling layouts, utilizing Toast messages, implementing onClickListeners, and more. Additionally, it afforded me the opportunity to gain proficiency in utilizing the Calendar Instance and manipulating date-related operations.

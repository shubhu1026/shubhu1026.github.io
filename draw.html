Kids Drawing App

An Android app that allows you to draw on a blank canvas or a favourite image from your device's storage and share it with others.

This application offers users the ability to sketch on either a blank canvas or an image sourced from their device's storage. Working on this project, I gained invaluable insights into the practical use of frame layouts, the manipulation of Canvas, effective path management, permissions handling, and the seamless import and export of images on Android devices.
